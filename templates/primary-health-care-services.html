SGBV, SRHR and Teenage pregnancies

We mostly focusing on the critical issues of child sexual based violence which includes: Teenage pregnancies, STIs and SRHR to the youth ranging 13 – 25th Years Old and this is mostly located in the High Schools and Universities;

Imbonizarwo organization focusing on educating teenagers to prevent health critical issues through identify the situation which can resulted into sexual based violence, ways of reporting the situation with facts, we educated them on the sexual reproductive health, contraceptive methods and preventions of associated risks in case they get into violence within recommended hours of avoiding pregnancies, transmission of HIV/AIDS

We implement the above programs through practical strategies of using artist with music containing educative message, Poems and dramatic performances that can easily attract and capture the interest of teenagers

Fighting drug abuse

The Imbonizarwo Organization implements this programs through working with people who get involved in drug consumption, drugs dealers towards educating young generations on the impacts of drugs and how they destroy lives; This is than through creative arts for campaigning the positive changes in the community and sharing facts on drugs

Our expected end results are to develop a transformative generation with no drugs who will be capable of safeguarding the mental wellbeing of all Rwandans through prevention, sustainable campaign on mental health education, and possibly working with rehabilitation center and support to those with mental illness due to the consumption of drugs.

Imbonizarwo Organization is open to work with government institutions which are focusing on this critical issues towards eradicating its under triple partnership of Community Mental Rehabilitation Unit works, Civil Society Organization and Government as we as media.

As most of the young generation are attracted by the use of media, we also created a practical platform which help us to educates them and this is sustainable, consistency with focus positive change message.
